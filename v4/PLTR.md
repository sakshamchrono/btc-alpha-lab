# Palantir (PLTR) Fundamental Analysis

## Company Overview
Palantir Technologies Inc. (PLTR) is a software company specializing in big data analytics and artificial intelligence (AI). It serves both government and commercial sectors, with its core offerings including the Gotham and Foundry platforms, and more recently, the Artificial Intelligence Platform (AIP). AIP is designed to integrate AI with existing software, enhancing decision-making across sectors and evolving into an orchestration layer for "Agentic AI."
