*   **Analyst Sentiment:** Generally positive, with most analysts rating TEAM as 'buy' or 'outperform'.

**Next Earnings Report:** Expected around April 30, 2026, or May 7, 2026, for Q3 2026. Analysts project EPS of $0.84 to $1.33.
