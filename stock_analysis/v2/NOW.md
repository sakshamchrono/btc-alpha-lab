## 1. Fundamental Analysis

ServiceNow (NOW) demonstrates a fundamentally strong business with significant growth potential. The company has successfully expanded its product suite beyond traditional IT Service Management (ITSM) to encompass customer service, HR, and security operations, leading to a substantial increase in workflow and transaction volumes.

*   **Growth:** NOW is expected to experience robust growth, with revenue projected to increase by over 21% this year and nearly 18% next year. Earnings Per Share (EPS) growth is anticipated to be even more significant.
*   **Profitability:** The company boasts a high EBITDA/Sales ratio and impressive profit margins, which are among the highest in the market. Profit margins are growing, and ServiceNow exhibits healthy returns on assets, equity, and invested capital.
*   **Balance Sheet:** NOW maintains a strong financial position with a healthy debt-to-free cash flow ratio and a solid debt-to-equity ratio, indicating strong solvency and financial flexibility. The company generated strong cash flow in Q4 2025.
*   **Valuation:** Currently, the stock trades at a premium valuation, particularly when considering earnings multiples and the enterprise value to sales ratio. Some intrinsic value estimates suggest it may be slightly undervalued by approximately 9%, but overall, it is considered to have a high valuation.
*   **Recent Earnings (Q4 2025):** ServiceNow reported strong results, exceeding analyst expectations. Adjusted EPS came in at $0.92 (vs. $0.87-$0.89 estimate), and revenue grew 20.7% year-over-year to $3.57 billion (vs. $3.53-$3.56 billion estimate). Subscription revenue grew 21% YoY. The company also announced an additional $5 billion share repurchase authorization.
*   **Guidance:** The company's performance indicates a positive outlook, with strong growth anticipated for the coming periods.
*   **Key Risks:** High valuation multiples, potential for slower-than-anticipated subscription revenue growth, and increasing competition from major tech players (Microsoft, Atlassian, BMC, etc.).
